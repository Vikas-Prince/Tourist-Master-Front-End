Places To Visit In Visakhapatnam

1. RK Beach

Ramakrishna Beach

12Km from city center

One of the most popular beaches of Vishakapatnam, RK beach is a beach for to spend some relaxed time, enjoy strolls across the beach, sunbath and more.

2. Kailasagiri

Kailasagiri

15km from city center

Located in the scenic locales of Vizag, Kailash Giri is a beautiful hilltop park perched at a height of 360 ft.The vistas of the surroundings and the beach below.

2. Rushikonda Beach

Rushikonda Beach

21Km from city center

The Rushikonda Beach is a stunning shore located on the coast of the Bay of Bengal in Vishakhapatnam,Indian state of Andhra Pradesh. Which is in charge of this natural beauty.

4. Simhachalam Temple

Simhachalam Temple

9Km from city center

As per the belief of the Hindu religion, an east facing entrance brings luck and prosperity while the west one brings victory and luckily the Simhachalam temple faces west.

5. Katika Waterfalls

Katika Waterfalls

70km from city center

A distance of 100 steps and a trek of 2 km will take you to a place where a most enchanting sight of Katiki Waterfalls awaits for the tourists Nice place.

4. Dolphin's Nose

Dolphin's Nose

4Km from city center

Very similar to a dolphins nose, this 174 m high rocky headland is situated in the south of Vizag . The nose towards the Bay of Bengal giving it a rather fascinating look.

7. VUDA Park (Taraka Rama)

VUDA Park

14Km from city center

The Visakhapatnam Urban Development Authority or VUDA park, is a popular attraction in Visakhapatnam and a favorite amongst the tourists. One of its main attractions

8. Lambasinghi

Lambasinghi

78km from city center

Fondly called the ‘Kashmir of Andhra Pradesh’, Lambasingi is quaint hamlet nestled in Chintapalli Mandal in richly forested Arakku Valley at elevation of 1025 m above the sea level.

9. Bheemili Beach

Bheemili Beach

38Km from city center

The long beautiful and pristine Bheemunipatnam Beach is located at the assemblage of Gosthani River and the Bay of Bengal.It also houses the remains of a 17th century fort and cemetery,

10. Submarine Museum

Submarine Museum

12Km from city center

During your stroll on the RK Beach, or as you drive past the beach road, you might notice a rather elaborate collection of Submarines. Step down and stop to witness the only.

11. Indira Gandhi Park

Indira Gandhi

16km from city center

The Indira Gandhi Zoological Park is located amidst the Kambalakonda Reserve Forest in Visakhapatnam, Andhra Pradesh. Declared open to the public on 19 May 1977.

12. Matsyadarshini Aquarium

Matsyadarshini Aquarium

11Km from city center

An initiative of the Visakhapatnam Municipal Corporation,It is a beautiful aquarium and houses countless species of fresh and salt water marine life captured.
